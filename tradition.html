Antique houses
Antique houses in Aklan, notably in Kalibo and other heritage sites, are cultural gems reflecting the province's history. Despite Aklan's fame for festivals and natural beauty, these well-preserved structures showcase traditional Filipino architectural styles influenced by Spanish, Chinese, and indigenous designs. Examples include mansions along Veterans Avenue and heritage houses in Balete Park.

Fishing and farming
Fishing and farming are integral parts of Aklanon livelihoods. Aklanons have traditionally relied on the sea and land for sustenance, with fishing villages dotting the coastline and agricultural communities thriving in the rural areas. Traditional fishing methods and farming techniques are passed down through generations, preserving Aklan's agrarian heritage.
Cultural Dances and Music
Aklanons have a rich tradition of music and dance, which are often showcased during festivals and celebrations. Traditional dances like the "Binanog" and "Inagong" feature intricate movements accompanied by indigenous music played on traditional instruments like drums, gongs, and bamboo percussion.
Hospitality and Community Bonding
Aklanons are known for their warm hospitality and strong sense of community. Visitors to Aklan often experience the welcoming nature of the locals, who take pride in sharing their culture, food, and traditions with guests. Community gatherings, fiestas, and other social events play an essential role in strengthening bonds among Aklanon families and neighbors.
