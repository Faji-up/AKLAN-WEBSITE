Ati-Atihan
The Ati-Atihan Festival in Aklan, Philippines, is a lively celebration honoring the Santo Niño and the indigenous Ati people. It features vibrant street dancing, music, and elaborate costumes, with participants painting their bodies black and dancing energetically to drums.

Binirayan Festival
Celebrated in the town of Antique, which was once part of Aklan, this festival commemorates the arrival of the Bornean Datus and the merging of their culture with the native Ati people. It includes cultural presentations, street dancing, and a variety of traditional activities.
