Libacao Eco-Tourism Park
This park in Libacao features lush forests, refreshing waterfalls, and natural pools. Visitors can trek through the jungle, swim in the falls, and enjoy a peaceful getaway.
Boracay Island
Famous worldwide for its pristine white sand beaches and crystal-clear waters, Boracay is a must-visit destination. Enjoy activities like snorkeling, scuba diving, parasailing, and island hopping. Don't miss the vibrant nightlife at White Beach and Bulabog Beach.
Bakhawan Eco-Park
Known as the Philippines' mangrove reforestation project, Bakhawan Eco-Park in Kalibo offers a peaceful retreat amid mangrove forests, wooden walkways, and diverse bird species. Visitors can enjoy kayaking, birdwatching, and educational tours.

Aklan Cathedral (St. John the Baptist Cathedral)
Located in Kalibo, this centuries-old cathedral is an architectural marvel and a significant religious site in the province.
Nabaoy River and Akean River
Perfect for adrenaline junkies, these rivers in Aklan offer thrilling activities like river tubing, kayaking, and bamboo rafting amidst scenic landscapes.
